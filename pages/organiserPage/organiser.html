How to Organize

To get started with organizing events or packages, simply follow these steps:

  • Sign Up / Log In: Click on "Get Started" to sign up or log in to your organizer account.
  • Add Events: Click "Organize Events" to fill out the form with details about your event services.
  • Add Packages: Click "Organize Packages" to create detailed listings for your packages.

Importance of Customer Satisfaction

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring a high level of service from all our organizers. Any complaints or issues reported by customers will be reviewed, and necessary actions will be taken, including the potential removal of organizer accounts.

How to Be a Good Organizer

To succeed as an organizer on our platform:

  • Provide accurate and detailed information about your services.
  • Maintain high standards of customer service and professionalism.
  • Respond to customer inquiries promptly and courteously.
  • Ensure all services meet the expectations set by your listings.
